# GDSR Studio

An OBS-style recording studio that runs inside Geometry Dash. No external software, no alt-tabbing. Press F8 and record.

**What you get:**

- Live preview of what's being recorded
- H.264 (x264 / NVENC / AMF / QSV) or MJPEG
- 3 audio tracks: game, mic, and game+mic mixed
- Audio mixer with per-source volume, mute and peak meters
- Performance presets from Potato to Quality
- Capture runs off the render thread, so the game stays smooth
- 15 languages, dark and light themes

Needs Geode for Geometry Dash 2.2081 (Windows). Press F8 in-game to open the studio.
